Restoring Hope, Rebuilding Lives

At Promises Of Restoration Centre, we believe that no one is beyond the reach of transformation. Located in Kiambu Kirigiti, our center is a strictly Christian-based non-profit dedicated to helping individuals and families break free from the cycle of alcohol and drug addiction.

We understand that addiction is a treatable disease that affects the whole person—body, mind, and soul. Our mission is to provide a safe, spiritual, and cost-effective environment where healing can begin. Through comprehensive counseling and evidence-based treatment, we walk alongside our clients with Integrity, Loyalty, and Honesty.

Why Choose Promises Of Restoration Centre?

Faith-Centered Healing

Our foundation is built on steadfast Christian principles, offering a supportive spiritual anchoring.

Affordable Care

As a non-profit, we prioritize your long-term recovery over profit, making treatment accessible.

Holistic Approach

We address the physical, spiritual, and emotional health of both the individual and their beloved family.

Our Vision

To free affected individuals and their families from the bonds of alcohol and drug addiction.

Our Mission

To improve the physical, spiritual, and emotional health of those suffering from addiction and their families. We provide safe, transformative, cost-effective, and comprehensive treatment and counseling, guided by integrity and the belief that addiction is a treatable disease.

Our Services

Psychological Counselling

Confidential one-on-one sessions focused on addressing the emotional, psychological, and behavioral factors contributing to addiction or mental health challenges.

Depression & Trauma

Specialized care and supportive interventions designed to heal deep emotional wounds and manage depressive symptoms.

Group Therapy

Professionally guided group sessions where individuals share experiences, build connections, and develop effective coping strategies in a supportive environment.

Overcoming Addiction

Comprehensive strategies and actionable steps to break free from substance dependency and reclaim a healthy, fulfilling life.

Medical Detoxification

Safe, medically supervised withdrawal from drugs or alcohol in a controlled environment, ensuring patient comfort and safety throughout the detox process.

Inpatient / Residential Rehabilitation

24/7 structured care in a safe and supportive setting, ideal for individuals requiring intensive treatment and close medical supervision.

Outpatient Programs

Flexible treatment options that allow individuals to receive therapy and support while continuing to live at home and maintain daily responsibilities.

Relapse Prevention Planning

Personalized strategies and practical tools to help individuals maintain long-term sobriety and confidently navigate life after treatment.

Occupational Therapy

Support in regaining independence by rebuilding essential daily living and work-related skills for a smooth return to normal routines.

Aftercare Management

Ongoing support through follow-ups, counseling, and support groups to ensure sustained recovery and successful reintegration into society.
